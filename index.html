Our Story

Born from a passion
for the perfect cup.

KŌHI began in a small roastery in Hyderabad with a simple belief: exceptional coffee demands exceptional care at every step — from the farmer's hands to your cup.

We partner directly with farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra, paying above fair-trade prices and co-investing in sustainable growing practices.

How We Work

From seed to cup —
every step matters.

01
🌱
Direct Sourcing
We travel to origin farms, building direct relationships with farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.
02
🔬
Quality Selection
Only specialty-grade lots scoring 85+ on the SCA scale make the cut. Our Q-graders taste hundreds of samples each season.
03
🔥
Small-Batch Roasting
Each origin is roasted to its ideal profile in 12kg batches. We roast twice weekly to ensure peak freshness on arrival.
04
📦
Swift Delivery
Shipped within 48 hours of roasting in valve-sealed bags to preserve aroma. Pan-India delivery in 3–5 business days.
